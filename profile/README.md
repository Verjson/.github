## What we do

verJSON Inc is an **AI R&D accelerator**. We treat the act of building and delivering
software as the experiment itself — asking how artificial intelligence can change every
stage of that process, then shipping real products to find out.

Our work runs on a tight loop:

```
research  →  build  →  deliver  →  learn  →  (repeat)
```

What works becomes an accelerator for the next experiment. What doesn't teaches us where
to look next.

## Focus areas

| | |
|---|---|
| 🧠 **AI-native development** | How agents, models, and tooling reshape the way software is designed, written, and reviewed. |
| 🚀 **Delivery & velocity** | Compressing the path from prototype to production with AI in the loop on testing, ops, and release. |
| 🧩 **AI-leveraged products** | Building products that put AI to work for real users — and learning what actually creates value. |
| ⚙️ **Accelerators & tooling** | Reusable building blocks that let the next experiment start further down the road. |
| 🔬 **Applied research** | Rigorous, hands-on experiments measured by what ships — not by what sounds good. |
| 🤝 **Human + AI workflows** | Designing collaboration between people and AI so each does what it does best. |

## How we work

1. **Ask a sharp question** — a concrete bet about how AI could change how a product gets built or delivered.
2. **Build to find out** — ship a real product or tool, the fastest and most honest way to test the hypothesis.
3. **Measure & compound** — fold what we learn back into the tools that power the next experiment.
